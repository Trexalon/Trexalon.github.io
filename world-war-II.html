World War II

Introduction

World War II began in Europe in 1939, when Germany invaded Poland. Great Britian and France responed by declaring war on Germany on September 3. The war was between the U.S.S.R and Germany on June 22, 1941, with operation Barbarossa, the German invasion of the Soviet Union.

Location

The name "World War" means a war thats across the globe(or at least most of it). Most of the war took place in Europe, east Asia, and the islands in the Pasific Ocean, but some took places in places such as Madagascar and the Aleutian Ilands.

Teams

The teams of World War II were two teams, the Axis Powers and the Allies. On the Axis Powers; the countrys were Germany, Italy, and Japan, and the Allies; the countrys were France, Great Britain, the Untitled States, the Soviet Union, and, to a lesser extent, China.

End of War
